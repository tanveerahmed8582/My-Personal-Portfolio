About Me

Hello! My name is Tanveer Ahmed, and I am from Mairwa, Siwan, Bihar. I recently completed my B.Tech in Computer Science Engineering with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence from Khwaja Moinuddin Chishti Language University, Lucknow.
As a frontend developer, I am passionate about designing and developing interactive and responsive web applications. My technical proficiency includes a strong command of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, along with hands-on experience in modern frontend frameworks such as React.
In addition to my technical skills, I am familiar with version control systems like Git and build tools like Webpack, which streamline my development process.
Thank you for taking the time to learn about me. I am excited about the opportunity to contribute to innovative projects and bring ideas to life as a frontend developer.
